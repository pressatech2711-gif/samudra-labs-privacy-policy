Privacy Policy — Kathakaar

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Kathakaar is a mythology storytelling app developed by Samudra Labs. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Kathakaar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information to function.

Device Permissions

Kathakaar does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or any other sensitive device permissions.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All app content is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Kathakaar does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no mechanisms for data collection from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we introduce features that involve data collection in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
